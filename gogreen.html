Ways to Go Green at the Office

18th Aug 2020

        In the world of office technology, environmental sustainability can be achieved in a number of ways and here are a few simple ways reduce your carbon (CO2)footprint and your bottom line at the office

“Big Changes Start with Small Steps.” - Unknown

Reduce Paper Waste:

      Print single sided double sided when you can. Duplex printing is a feature of some computer printers and multifunction printers that allows the printing of a sheet of paper on both sides. Duplex capable devices can easily be programmed to print double sided by changing the print settings from your computer.

      Explore pull printing. Pull Printing is a printing feature where a user's print job is held on a server or on a user's workstation and released by the user at any printing device which supports this feature. To utilize pull printing, users must be authenticated at the printer using either embedded software or an external device based on the capabilities of your machine. Users then select from a list of print jobs upon arriving at a printer. The advantages are not only cost effective and environmentally friendly; it also creates flexibility, security and management of print jobs.
